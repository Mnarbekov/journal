**Status:** candidate — not yet decided

What if, alongside the usual weekly check-in, I kept a short list of *friction points* — the small things that caused unnecessary delay or frustration during the week? Not complaints, just patterns.

The hypothesis: if the same item shows up three weeks running, it's worth solving properly rather than pushing through each time. If it never repeats, it was noise.

Format would be minimal: one line per item, date, optional note. Could live in the journal or in its own recurring note.

Open questions:
- Would I actually maintain this, or add it to the "good idea, abandoned in week two" pile?
- Might be more useful as a fortnightly cadence to avoid nit-picking every small hiccup.
